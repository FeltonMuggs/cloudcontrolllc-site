The loop

Register once. Set your terms. Get paid when your data creates value.

Cloud vaults still ask you to trust a company's servers. The DNaI Genomic Wallet removes that trust — the sequence sits on silicon you physically control.

01 · REGISTER

Seal your genome

Load your sequencing file onto the wallet. It's encrypted to the on-device vault instantly and hashed locally — only the hash and a pointer are registered.

02 · CONSENT

Set license terms

Pick who may access what, for which purpose, at what royalty. Consent is machine-readable, revocable, and signed by the device's secure element.

03 · QUERY

Answered on-device

A licensee's approved query runs inside the wallet against your vault. Only the consented result is returned. Your genome never transits the network.

04 · SETTLE

You get paid

The royalty splits automatically to the wallet keys only you hold, with an immutable receipt. Income shows on the device and your dashboard.

05 · REVOKE

Change your mind, anytime

Press the hardware consent button to revoke. Future queries are refused and the on-chain state proves your revocation.

06 · CONTROL

Sovereign by design

Keys are generated on-chip and never exportable. No personal data ever goes on-chain — only hashes and consent state.

Architecture

A hardware vault, built on Raspberry Pi

Five layers turn a Pi into a sovereign genomic appliance. Raw data has no network egress path by design.

LAYER 1 · STORAGE

Encrypted vault

Full-disk-encrypted NVMe holds your VCF / CRAM / FASTQ. Unsealed only after the secure element attests the device. Read only by local compute.

LAYER 2 · CONSENT

Consent-as-code engine

Revocable, royalty-aware consent runs on the device. Every grant or revocation is hardware-signed; only its hash and state reach the chain.

LAYER 3 · KEYS

Secure-element custody

A hardware secure element holds your royalty wallet key and vault master key. Keys are generated on-chip and never leave it.

LAYER 4 · COMPUTE-TO-DATA

On-device query gateway

Approved licensee queries and model inference execute locally against the vault. Only the consented result is returned and billed.

LAYER 5 · ON-CHAIN CLIENT

Registry & royalty rail

A thin client publishes asset hashes and pointers, syncs consent, and receives royalty settlements — testnet first, mainnet when compliance clears.

THE PROMISE

No raw genome transits the network. No personal data on-chain. Monetization without data transfer — the cleanest answer to privacy law.
