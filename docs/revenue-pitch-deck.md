## Slide 1 — The One-Liner

We tokenize genome ownership, enforce consent on-chain with ZK proofs, and run a marketplace where pharma and AI companies pay genome holders directly for authorized access. DNaI takes a 20% protocol fee on every transaction.

Target state: 10,000 genome holders live · 6 paying enterprise customers · ~$2M ARR · growing ~3x quarter-over-quarter.

## Slide 6 — Pricing Power

Pharma pays under 3% of the value the data unlocks. Distribution per standard transaction: 73% holder / 20% protocol / 7% DAO treasury. Priced at a 150x premium over the 2018 23andMe/GSK deal, justified by on-chain consent, holder compensation, ZK verification, and real-time access.
