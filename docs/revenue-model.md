## Branch 3: Tokenized Marketplace Mechanics

### Marketplace Architecture

**Supply Side (Genome Holders)**
- List specific data scopes for access: FULL_GENOME, VARIANT_PANEL, DISEASE_RISK, DRUG_RESPONSE, ANCESTRY
- Set price floors and access duration
- Opt into subscription pools (recurring access = recurring income)
- Accept/reject individual access requests via wallet notification

**Demand Side (Buyers: Pharma, Research, AI)**
- Browse available genomic data by scope, ancestry, condition, cohort size
- Submit access requests with price and duration
- Purchase subscription pool access for ongoing research
- License curated datasets for AI model training

### Pricing Engine
- **Base rate:** $75–$500/genome/month depending on scope (VARIANT_PANEL cheapest, FULL_GENOME most)
- **Cohort premium:** 2× multiplier for specific variant cohorts (rare variants, specific ancestry)
- **Longitudinal premium:** 1.5× for multi-year access agreements
- **Auction clearing:** High-demand variant panels cleared by Dutch auction
- **Dynamic floor:** Protocol adjusts floor price based on 30-day demand signal

### Marketplace Fee Structure
| Transaction Type | Protocol Fee | Holder Receive | DAO Treasury |
|----------------|-------------|---------------|-------------|
| Standard access grant | 20% | 73% | 7% |
| Auction clearing | 25% | 68% | 7% |
| AI training license | 30% | 63% | 7% |
| Subscription pool | 18% | 75% | 7% |

### Revenue from Marketplace (Phase III Projection)
- 10,000 genomes × 30% participation rate = 3,000 active listings
- Average access price: $150/genome/month
- Monthly GMV: $450,000
- Protocol revenue (20%): $90,000/month

## Branch 4: AI and Computation Framework

### AI Revenue Streams

**Model Training Licenses**
- AI companies pay for access to consented genomic datasets for model training
- License terms: per-epoch, per-run, or perpetual for specific model version
- Pricing: $5,000–$50,000 per training dataset license
- Consent enforcement: smart contract verifies license before ZK-gated data release

**Federated Learning Infrastructure**
- Compute-over-data: AI model trains on encrypted vaults without decryption
- DNaI provides the orchestration layer; genome holders provide data + opt-in compute
- Revenue: 15% of compute fees + 20% of data access fees
- Partners: DeepMind (AlphaFold extensions), Tempus AI, Recursion

**Genomic Inference API**
- Pay-per-query access to DNaI-licensed genomic foundation models
- Pricing: $0.01–$0.10 per inference call
- Use cases: drug response prediction, disease risk scoring, ancestry inference
- Phase IV revenue potential: $50K–$200K/month at scale

**AI Model Marketplace**
- Third-party AI companies list their genomic models for licensing
- DNaI takes 15% marketplace fee
- Genome holders who contributed training data receive ongoing royalties from model usage

### Computation Framework Integration
- Week 20 (Phase IV): AI licensing marketplace MVP
- Federated learning pilot: 1 AI company in Phase III closed beta
- Inference API: public beta at Phase IV launch

## Branch 6: Sovereignty and Compliance

### Compliance as a Revenue Driver

**Compliance Certification Service**
- Certify pharma and research partners as "DNaI Consent Verified" — a trust mark
- Annual certification: $25,000–$100,000 depending on data volume
- Includes: smart contract audit of their access patterns, consent rate reports, ZK proof verification logs
- Cloud Control LLC GBA BMM certification positions us as credible issuer of this certification

**Audit Trail Product (SaaS)**
- Enterprise compliance teams subscribe to a compliance dashboard
- Real-time view of all consent grants, access events, revocations, royalty flows
- HIPAA and GDPR audit export (one-click regulatory reporting)
- Pricing: $2,000–$10,000/month per enterprise subscriber

**Regulatory Reporting Automation**
- Automated GDPR Article 30 (Records of Processing) generation from on-chain data
- HIPAA audit trail export for covered entity partners
- FDA 21 CFR Part 11 electronic records compliance for clinical trial genomic data
- Pricing: $500–$2,500/month per regulatory report type

**Cross-Jurisdiction Compliance Package**
- Enterprise clients operating in multiple jurisdictions buy a compliance bundle
- DNaI maintains the regulatory matrix; clients pay for updates and certifications
- Annual package: $50,000–$200,000 for 10+ jurisdiction coverage

### Compliance Revenue (Phase III Projection)
- 10 certified enterprise partners × $50K avg: $500K ARR
- 20 audit trail SaaS subscribers × $5K/month: $100K MRR
- 5 regulatory reporting subscriptions × $2K/month: $10K MRR
- Total compliance revenue: $1.1M ARR
